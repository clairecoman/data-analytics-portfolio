# 📊 Excel E-commerce Sales Dashboard

## 🧩 Business Problem
This project analyzes historical e-commerce sales data to identify revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and payment method performance. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into an interactive Excel dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making and improves business performance visibility.

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI metrics (Total Revenue, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Total Orders)
- Revenue trend over months
- Revenue by product category
- Revenue by region
- Payment method distribution
- Interactive slicers for filtering

## 🔎 Key Insights
- Revenue shows a gradual decline from January to June, indicating potential seasonal or demand shifts.
- Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category, contributing the most to overall revenue.
- Card payments dominate transactions, showing strong digital payment adoption among customers.
